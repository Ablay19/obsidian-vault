# Product Guide: AI-Powered Obsidian Automation Bot

## Initial Concept

The project is an AI-Powered Obsidian Automation Bot. It is a Go-based Telegram bot designed to serve as a powerful, AI-enhanced assistant for note-taking and knowledge management with Obsidian. The bot can process images and PDFs, extract text, and use Google's Gemini AI to summarize content, answer questions, and generate structured data. It offers an interactive, conversational experience directly within Telegram, including real-time streaming of AI responses. It also features a web dashboard for monitoring and management.

## Primary Goal

The primary goal of this bot is to provide a seamless and intelligent way for users to capture and organize notes from various sources, such as images, PDFs, and chat messages, directly into their Obsidian vault. This streamlines the process of knowledge management and reduces manual effort.

## Target Users

The bot is designed to cater to a diverse range of users who benefit from efficient knowledge management:

*   **Students and Researchers:** Individuals who need to quickly capture and organize information from academic papers, textbooks, and lecture notes for their studies and research.
*   **Content Creators and Writers:** Professionals who gather research, inspiration, and content ideas from various online and offline sources, requiring an organized system to manage their findings.
*   **Productivity Enthusiasts:** Anyone looking to enhance their personal knowledge management (PKM) workflow, seeking to automate data entry and categorization to improve overall productivity.

## Critical Features (Initial Version)

For the initial functional release, the bot will prioritize the following critical features:

*   **File Processing (Images & PDFs):** This core functionality enables the bot to ingest and extract relevant information from image files (using OCR) and PDF documents, forming the foundation for all subsequent AI processing.
*   **AI-Powered Summarization:** The bot will leverage advanced AI models to generate concise and accurate summaries of the extracted content, providing immediate value to users by condensing large amounts of information.
*   **Interactive Q&A:** Users will be able to engage with the bot by asking questions about the content of processed files, enhancing interaction and making the bot a more useful tool for information retrieval and understanding.

# Product Guidelines: AI-Powered Obsidian Automation Bot

## 1. Communication Style

The bot will maintain a **formal and professional** prose style in all its communications. This ensures clarity, builds trust, and aligns with the bot's role as a reliable productivity tool.

## 2. Visual Identity (Web Dashboard)

The web dashboard will adopt a **data-rich and utilitarian** design, presenting information densely for power users who need comprehensive monitoring. It will also be **dark mode-centric**, offering a sleek, dark-themed interface to reduce eye strain and cater to technical users' preferences.

## 3. Error Messages and User Feedback

Error messages and user feedback will be both **informative and solution-oriented**, clearly explaining problems and suggesting direct next steps or solutions. Additionally, the tone will be **supportive and empathetic**, acknowledging user challenges and providing guidance in a reassuring manner.
